About-Us

Welcome to Universal label, your trusted partner in high-quality self-adhesive label manufacturing. With years of experience and innovation, we specialize in providing businesses across industries with top-of-the-line labeling solutions designed to meet your specific needs.
At Universal label, we are more than just a manufacturer. We are a team of experts committed to delivering excellence in every label we produce. From small businesses to large enterprises, we serve a wide range of clients, offering customized, durable, and visually appealing labels that make your products stand out.

Universal labels works with clients to build long-term relationships and believes in offering the right solution as per the business needs of its clients. Universal labels with its infrastructure, expertise, experience, and knowledge offers its clients printing services along with printing solutions. Our labelling solutions are developed to match the distinct needs of many industry verticals including homecare, personal care, food and beverage, lubricant, pharmaceutical, agro-chemicals, to name a few.
